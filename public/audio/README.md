# Audio Assets

## Music Files (place in this directory)

For better quality audio, download free music and sound effects from:
- **Freesound.org** - https://freesound.org
- **OpenGameArt.org** - https://opengameart.org
- **Incompetech.com** - https://incompetech.com (Kevin MacLeod's music, CC-BY)
- **Purple Planet** - https://www.purple-planet.com

### Suggested Music Files:
1. `menu-theme.mp3` - Ambient menu music (calm, mysterious)
2. `dungeon-theme.mp3` - Dark, ominous dungeon music
3. `space-theme.mp3` - Atmospheric space/sci-fi music
4. `mansion-theme.mp3` - Eerie, haunting orchestral
5. `cyber-theme.mp3` - Electronic, cyberpunk beats

### Suggested Sound Effects:
1. `click.mp3` - UI click sound
2. `vote.mp3` - Vote confirmation sound
3. `action.mp3` - Action submission sound
4. `success.mp3` - Success/positive feedback
5. `transition.mp3` - Screen transition whoosh
6. `notification.mp3` - Alert/notification sound
